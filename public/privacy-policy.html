Find My Puppy - COPPA Compliant Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025
Effective Date: January 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Find My Puppy! We are committed to protecting your privacy and the privacy of your children. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). This policy is designed to comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect the following information:

  • Email Address: Used for account identification and communication
  • Password: Securely encrypted using bcrypt hashing algorithm (one-way encryption) - we cannot see or retrieve your actual password
  • Username: A display name you choose for your account (not your real name)
  • Google Account Information (if using Google Sign-In): Email, name, and profile picture (only if you choose to sign in with Google)

2.2 Game Progress Data

We collect the following game-related information to provide and improve our services:

  • Level progress (levels completed in Easy, Medium, and Hard difficulties)
  • Game scores and points earned
  • Hints purchased and used
  • Premium subscription status
  • Purchase history (for in-app purchases via Razorpay)
  • Referral codes used (if you were referred by another user)

2.3 Technical Information

We automatically collect certain technical information to ensure the App functions properly:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version and usage statistics
  • Last activity timestamp (to track online status for game features)
  • Last login timestamp
  • Error logs and crash reports (to fix bugs and improve the App)

2.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • Real names or physical addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Location data (GPS coordinates)
  • Photos or videos from your device
  • Contact lists or address books
  • Biometric data
  • Credit card information (handled securely by Razorpay payment processor)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: To create and manage your account, authenticate logins, and provide customer support
  • Game Functionality: To save your game progress, track scores, unlock levels, and provide hints
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, and enhance the gaming experience
  • Communication: To send important updates about the App, security notifications, and respond to your inquiries
  • Payment Processing: To process in-app purchases through Razorpay (we do not store payment card information)
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Encryption

We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data:

  • Password Encryption: All passwords are hashed using bcrypt (one-way encryption) before storage. We cannot see or retrieve your actual password.
  • Data Transmission: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Database Security: User data is stored in MongoDB Atlas with encryption at rest

4.2 Storage Location

Your data is stored on secure servers provided by MongoDB Atlas, which may be located in the United States or other countries. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data regardless of storage location.

4.3 Security Measures

We implement multiple layers of security to protect your information:

  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls limiting who can view user data
  • Monitoring for unauthorized access attempts
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Encrypted database connections

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

5.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it.

5.2 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may have access to certain information:

  • Google Authentication: If you choose to sign in with Google, Google processes your authentication. We only receive your email, name, and profile picture. Google's privacy policy applies to their services.
  • Razorpay (Payment Processing): For in-app purchases, payment information is processed by Razorpay. We do not store your credit card or payment details. Razorpay's privacy policy applies to payment transactions.
  • MongoDB Atlas (Database Hosting): Our database is hosted on MongoDB Atlas, which provides secure cloud storage. MongoDB's privacy policy applies to their hosting services.

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government regulation, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

üîí COPPA Compliance: This App is designed for children under 13. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent.

6.1 Parental Consent

If a child under 13 wishes to create an account, we require verifiable parental consent before collecting any personal information. Parents can:

  • Review the information collected from their child
  • Request deletion of their child's account and data
  • Refuse to allow further collection or use of their child's information

6.2 Limited Data Collection for Children

For children under 13, we only collect the minimum information necessary to provide the App's functionality:

  • Email address (with parental consent)
  • Username (not their real name)
  • Game progress data

6.3 Parental Rights

Parents have the right to:

  • Review their child's personal information
  • Delete their child's account and data
  • Revoke consent and refuse further collection
  • Request a copy of their child's data

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or regulatory purposes (such as purchase history for accounting and tax compliance).

8. Your Rights and Choices

8.1 Account Deletion

You can request deletion of your account at any time by contacting us at the email address provided below. Upon account deletion:

  • Your account information will be permanently deleted
  • Your game progress will be erased
  • Your purchase history will be anonymized (for legal/accounting purposes)

8.2 Access and Correction

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request a copy of your data

8.3 GDPR Rights (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification (correction)
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

8.4 CCPA Rights (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know if your personal information is sold or disclosed (we do not sell your data)
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (not applicable - we do not sell data)
  • Access your personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an email notification (if you have provided your email)
  • Displaying a notice in the App

Your continued use of the App after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
